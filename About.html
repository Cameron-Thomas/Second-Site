About Us

From our humble beginnings in 2013 as a one-room, one-man operation, we’ve grown into one of Southwest Missouri’s most beloved bicycle shops! Located in Springfield, Missouri, we are proud to be authorized dealers of Cannondale, Orbea, and Scott bikes! We carry a full line of clothing, gear, and accessories, including brands such as Shimano, SRAM, Louis Garneau, Pearl Izumi, Sugoi, and more. With a wide range of manufacturer partnerships throughout the cycling industry, we can accommodate almost any request or order. And if your current ride needs a little TLC, a tune up, a complete overhaul, or anything in between, our full-service repair shop is capable of putting your bike back in like-new condition. Want to customize your current bike, or build a new one to your own specs? We’ve got you covered! Need to ship a bike? We can do that as well. Whatever your cycling needs may be, our top-notch expert staff will take care of you with the same exceptional customer service and care that has made us the “go to” choice of cyclists throughout Southwest Missouri!
